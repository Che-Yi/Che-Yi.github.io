ABOUT

Che-Yi is a master's student in the department of Industrial & Operations Engineering at the University of Michigan. He is especially interested in simulation, stochastic optimization, and their applications in healthcare system and risk management. As a graduate research assistant in the Center for Healthcare Engineering & Pateint Safety (CHEPS), his research work primarily involves simulation in medical settings to help support decision-making. Apart from the work in healthcare system, Che-Yi also has research experiences in revenue management.
